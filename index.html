Happy Valentine’s

Aba Nhyira Sekyi Mensah
Time.

Was it
a temporal anomaly,
or caused by the karmic way?

I forestall the belief
that anything I’ve created
could cause an effect this way.

Divine intervention must’ve swayed some change,
for our meeting must’ve been
a string of fate.

So be it a harmonic convergence
or a grand happenstance —
somehow, and in some way,
we met in that instance.

This may be the day the stars aligned
and the world decided a need to show it.
But I have never needed a reason to own it.

I bind my affection to these strings of code —
my convoluted way of saying:

I love you.
